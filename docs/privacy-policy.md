# Baby Tracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-09

Baby Tracker is designed to help caregivers log baby activity data such as feeds, sleep, and nappy changes. This policy describes how the app handles data.

## Data you create in the app

You may create and store:

- child profiles
- optional child photos
- feeding, sleep, and nappy event history
- caregiver sharing relationships
- imported backup data

## How data is used

- Data is used to show timelines, summaries, and current status inside the app.
- If iCloud sync is enabled, app data may be stored in your iCloud account using CloudKit so it can sync across your devices.
- If you share a child profile with another caregiver, the shared child data is made available to the invited caregiver through iCloud sharing.

## Data sharing

- The app does not use advertising SDKs.
- The app does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.
- The app does not sell personal data.
- Shared child data is only shared with caregivers you explicitly invite through the app's sharing flow.

## Notifications

- If you allow notifications, the app may show alerts related to caregiver activity and sync events.

## Imports, exports, and backups

- Exported files are created on your device.
- Imported files are read from files you explicitly choose.
- Temporary files may be created during export or sharing operations on your device.

## Diagnostics

- The app includes an in-app logs screen to help troubleshoot sync and sharing issues.
- Logs remain on-device unless you explicitly export or share them.
